General Questions

1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, open the app and explore the wide selection of restaurants and menu items. You can browse by cuisine, restaurant name, or even specific dishes. Once you’ve made your selections, add the items to your cart. When you’re ready, head to the cart and review your order. You’ll then proceed to checkout, where you can enter your delivery details, choose a payment method, and confirm the order. After confirmation, the restaurant will begin preparing your food.


2. How can I track my order?

Once you’ve placed your order, you can track its progress in real time. Simply go to the "Orders" section in the app, where you’ll see a live status of your current order. The tracking will let you know when the restaurant starts preparing your meal, when a delivery partner picks it up, and when it’s en route to your location. This way, you can monitor every step and be updated on when to expect your delivery.


3. What if my food is late or hasn’t arrived?

In case your food is delayed or hasn't arrived within the expected delivery window, you can first check the real-time tracking for updates. If the tracking shows no progress or if there's a significant delay, you can directly contact the restaurant via the "Help" button on the order details page. Alternatively, you can reach out to our dedicated customer support team through the in-app chat or phone line. Our team will assist you in resolving the issue or provide further assistance, such as processing a refund if necessary.


4. Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it?

Once an order is placed, there is a limited time during which you can cancel or make changes to it. If the restaurant hasn’t yet started preparing your food, you can cancel the order by navigating to the "Orders" section, selecting the active order, and tapping "Cancel." After the restaurant begins cooking, the cancellation feature will no longer be available. Any successful cancellations will be followed by a refund, and you’ll receive a confirmation email or notification with the details.
